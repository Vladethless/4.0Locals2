    DOUBLE-HUNG WINDOWS

    HOME/DOUBLE-HUNG WINDOWS

    Windows are another part of your house, and you may not have realized that they play a significant role in your houseâ€™s overall look. We all want a place that's not only cozy and comfortable but also modern and pleasing to the eyes. That is why the windows you choose need to be the perfect balance between contemporary and functional to give your house the uplift it needs.

    A great option in that regard is double-hung windows. These are two slidable vertical windows that let air in through the top and the bottom. Unlike its counterpart, single-hung windows only allow one part to slide over the others while the other is fixed.

    Are These Windows Expensive?

    Since these windows work on a different mechanism than the other types of windows, such as sliding ones, they are slightly more expensive and high maintenance. The cost is not extremely high, but there are cheaper options if you are looking for budget-friendly windows.

    Why Should You Opt For These?

    In addition to the fact that these are super modern and high-end, they also have more functionality and efficiency. There is a great way to up your ventilation game all around the house. Furthermore, they provide the most beautiful look to your home while letting plenty of light in to save your energy consumption too.

    Maintaining Double-Hung Windows

    Many individuals are not affected by the cost or design but are only looking for a choice that is functional and will get the job without as little effort as possible. These elegant-looking casements do require some maintenance depending on the type of casements you install and what glass you opt to use in them. They can easily be repainted in different colors after a few years of installation to refresh the outlook of the house. Regular cleaning and service are a must with almost all casement types to ensure they are working correctly.
